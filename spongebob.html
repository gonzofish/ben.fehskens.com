Fun Facts

  • SpongeBob was almost named SpongeBoy — the name was trademarked by a mop company.
  • Creator Stephen Hillenburg had a marine biology degree and taught ocean science before making the show.
  • The show premiered on May 1, 1999 — over 25 years ago!
  • Bikini Bottom is loosely based on Bikini Atoll, a real island in the Pacific Ocean.
  • SpongeBob's pet snail Gary meows like a cat.
  • Sandy Cheeks is from Texas and holds multiple in-universe patents.
  • Tom Kenny has voiced SpongeBob for the entire run of the show.
  • Hillenburg originally wanted the show to end after the first movie in 2004 — Nickelodeon had other plans.

Favorite Episodes

  1. Band Geeks (S2E15)
    Squidward's band finally gets their moment. Widely considered the greatest episode ever made.
  2. Chocolate with Nuts (S3E11)
    SpongeBob and Patrick try to sell chocolate bars door-to-door. "CHOCOLATE!!!!"
  3. The Camping Episode (S3E17)
    SpongeBob and Patrick set up camp three feet from the house. Features the legendary Sea Bear song.
  4. Graveyard Shift (S2E16)
    SpongeBob and Squidward work the night shift alone. Nosferatu.
  5. Krusty Krab Training Video (S2E12)
    A mock corporate training video for new Krabby Patty employees. Peak fourth-wall energy.
